Let's talk for a minute...or 30...or 60...or 120.

We have two main types of consultations—popups and private.

Our "popups" happen once a month at a specified location; they're first come, first serve, for whomever is interested. At only $1 per minute, it's setup to be low-risk to potential clients and also a chance for entrepreneurs new to consulting to try it out.

Our private consultations are as short as 30-minutes and can go as long as two hours. They can range from project discussions to growth strategy to code reviews to design feedback, etc.
